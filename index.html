Biography

DJ Tahwiao is a Melbourne based DJ and Producer. Born a half-caste Maori in Wanganui, New Zealand to a fundamentalist Christian family, 'Eff' Alexander grew up isolated from the world of music. During his formative years he would develop a hidden love for electronic music, rebelling against his parents and religious creed.

Eff is a passionate artist, something his productions and DJ sets make very clear. His selections and mixing technique vary from set to set with a mentality that is unwilling to be defined by a singular sound.
